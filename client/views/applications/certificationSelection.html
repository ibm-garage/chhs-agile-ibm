SWaM

The SWaM certification program (Small, Women-owned, and Minority-owned Business) is a state program of the Commonwealth of Virginia. The purpose is to enhance procurement opportunities for SWaM businesses participating in state-funded projects. When applying for this certification, it is required to specify which designation you wish to apply.
